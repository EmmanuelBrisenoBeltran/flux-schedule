FluxOps & Deployment Guide

Follow these steps to schedule your team, assign positions, and build the logistical parking deployments for Intuit Dome and Sofi Stadium.

1
Review the Dashboard & Roster Before assigning an event, check the Dashboard and Daily Roster to see which staff members are marked as Available, Unavailable, or on a Blackout Day.
2
Build Event Shifts Navigate to the Events tab. Find a Pending event and click Build Shifts. Here you define exactly what time blocks (Shift Hours) are needed (e.g., 4:00 PM, 7:00 PM).
3
Assign the General Staff Roster Once shifts are built, click View/Assign on the event. Inside, you can assign users to the shift blocks. The system will warn you ⚠️ if you select a user that is unavailable or blacked out for that date. (Hold Cmd/Ctrl to select multiple users at once).
4
Map Deployments (Parking Zones) Go to the Deployments tab. Click Map Deployments on your event.
  • Step 1: Select which parking zones and granular sub-locations (like West - Prairie (Ramp)) are active during each specific shift hour.
  • Step 2: Allocate the staff pool (from Step 3) into their physical parking zone locations.
5
Send Automated Logistics Emails Once everything is saved, open the ✓ Completed section in the Deployments tab and click the ✉️ Gmail button layout. The system will automatically draft an email outlining the Shift/Location architecture and BCC all Managers/Leads assigned to the event. Check your Gmail Drafts to send!
